Fire360 Preview

Reliability benchmark for degraded 360° firefighting scenarios.

Paper (soon) Code (soon) Dataset (soon)

Motivation

Firefighting scenes push perception to its limits — smoke, heat, and motion everywhere. 360° vision captures full situational context where reliability saves lives.

Key Insight

VQA under degradation: humans stay ≈ 90%, GPT-4o drops to single digits as smoke thickens and light fades.

What’s Inside

  • Professionally recorded 360° firefighter training videos
  • Tasks across perception, reasoning, and episodic memory
  • Evaluation under smoke, low light, thermal distortion, and occlusion
